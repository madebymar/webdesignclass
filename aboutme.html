Get to Know Me!




My name is Mariana Barros, I am currently a student at Arizona State University majoring in Design Studies.
I have an educational background and professional experience in architectural design.

While studying this, I gained an extensive graphic, video, and animation skillset and I now specialize in videography and graphic design. I've always been a storyteller first, long before I discovered videography. It all started with constantly writing silly imaginary pieces when I was a kid, until the day I picked up a camera and discovered another medium to express myself. My favorite thing in the world to do is capture authentic moments and turn them into a film that will last forever.

It's no surprise that videography is the love of my life, but traveling is just as important to me. I'm endlessly inspired by new adventures, nature, and cultures. I love waking up to new horizons, making new friends, and exploring what this world has to offer.
When I'm not at my computer editing, designing, or out and about filming, you'll find me playing my guitar or piano, dancing salsa all night, or binge-watching netflix.
